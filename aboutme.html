My Background

I’m Richard A. Jegede, and I believe that for a system to truly work, you have to understand both the hardware and the heart of the data it produces. I grew up in Nigeria, and to be honest, life wasn’t easy. Losing my parents at a very young age could have broken me, but I chose a different path. I chose education as my weapon. I fought to earn my Higher National Diploma in Mechatronics Engineering from The Polytechnic Ibadan, focusing on the things I love: Control Systems, Robotics, and Electrical Engineering. My time on the factory floor as an intern at Chivita Plc changed everything for me. Working with Siemens systems, automotive machines, and industrial safety (HSE) taught me how machines breathe. But I also saw a gap—I realized that data is the key to making those machines smarter. That’s why I didn't stop at my degree. I pushed myself to master Data Analysis and AI through intensive bootcamps and certifications. Today, I’m an engineer who can code in Python and SQL, build Power BI dashboards, and use Machine Learning to solve real-world engineering problems. I’m not just looking for a job; I’m looking for a mission where I can use my grit and my dual-skills to improve productivity, stop machines from breaking down, and help a company grow. I’ve never given up on life, and I won’t give up on finding the best solution for your team.
